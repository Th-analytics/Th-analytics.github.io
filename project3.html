Housing Price Prediction


In today's world business can expand itself by gaining insight into it's requirement by tapping into it's own raw data using Machine Learning.

  • This will help them to forecast its customer behaviours.

  • Will help into segment out customers.

  • This will decrease the overall expenditure of company.

  • Improve overall business operation and customer satisfaction.


Considering the business need this application is based on Housing Price Prediction in Boston using Boston area data.

Skills Demonstrated:

Python Programming, Data Cleaning,FLASK,numpy, pandas, Linear Regression,Regularisation,Model Selection.



This model takes input of different parameters like population of the area, distance from sea, TAX and many other.

The output is in the form of Price in Dollars.
